Haiyang Liu

liuhaiyangkmj.iis.u-tokyo.ac.jp

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Information Science and Technology, The University of Tokyo, supervised by Prof. Shunsuke Kamijo, since 2020. I am working on generative model for cross-modal alignment, e.g., motion and audio. I received my M.E. from Waseda University, in 2020, and B.E. from Southeast University, in 2019.

I prefer and respect simple yet effective ideas, e.g., Associative Embedding, Motion Manifolds. I'm open for collaboration in content generation of the digital twins, virtual Youtuber and game NPC, etc.

Research interests

  • Computer Graphic: Character Animation
  • Human Motion Generation
  • Cross-Modal Alignment between Motion and Audio
  • Co-Speech Video Generation
